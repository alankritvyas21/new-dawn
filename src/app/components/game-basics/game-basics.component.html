New Dawn: Beyond the Fallout

You've survived the nuclear apocalypse. The wasteland awaits. Learn the basics before stepping into the unknown.

How to Play

  • 💬 Choice-Based Gameplay: Every decision shapes your fate.
  • ⚖️ Consequences Matter: Your actions influence your survival and ending.
  • 📦 Resource Management: Keep track of your food, water, and pills.
  • 🧬 Explore and Discover: Uncover secrets of NovaGen and the wasteland.

Survivor Tips

  • Conserve your resources — scarcity defines survival.
  • Not everyone can be trusted.
  • Sometimes doing nothing changes everything.
